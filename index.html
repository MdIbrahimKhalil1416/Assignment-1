Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DevConf 2026

Where will DevConf 2026 take place?

The conference is a hybrid event taking place live in San Francisco, CA and broadcast globally to virtual pass holders.

Are workshop seats guaranteed?

Pro and Team pass holders get priority access to book workshops. Standard pass holders can register 2 weeks prior to the event.

Is there a group discount?

Yes! Our Team package offers discounted access for up to 10 attendees along with premium corporate perks.

How do I access recorded sessions?

All session recordings will be uploaded to the attendee portal within 48 hours after the closing keynote.
